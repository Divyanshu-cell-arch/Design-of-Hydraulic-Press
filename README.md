# Design of a Hydraulic Press for Sheet Metal Forming

## Project Overview

This project presents the design and analysis of a compact **50 kN Hydraulic Press** for sheet metal forming applications. The press is designed to be cost-effective, structurally safe, and suitable for small workshops and educational laboratories. The project includes CAD modelling, engineering calculations, finite element analysis (FEA), and manufacturing drawings.

---

## Objectives

- Design a compact H-frame hydraulic press
- Achieve a pressing force of **50 kN**
- Ensure structural safety with FEA
- Minimize weight while maintaining strength
- Prepare manufacturing-ready CAD models and drawings

---

## CAD Design

The complete assembly consists of:

- H-Frame
- Upper & Lower Beams
- Hydraulic Cylinder
- Piston & Rod
- Table Plate
- Base Plate
- Fasteners
