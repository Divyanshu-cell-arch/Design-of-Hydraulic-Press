# Weight Estimation & Validation

## Objective

The weight estimation for each component of the hydraulic press was carried out to ensure that the overall machine remains close to the **200 kg** design constraint while maintaining adequate structural strength and functionality.

---

## Material Properties

- **Material:** Mild Steel
- **Density:** **0.00785 g/mm³** (7.85 g/cm³)

---

## 1. H-Frame

**Material:** Mild Steel

**Dimensions**

- Height = 1200 mm
- Length = 800 mm
- Thickness = 15 mm

**Volume Calculation**

```text
V = 1200 × 800 × 15

V = 14,400,000 mm³

V = 14.4 L
```

**Weight Calculation**

```text
Weight = 14,400,000 × 0.00785

Weight = 112,560 g

Weight = 112.56 kg
```

## Validation

- **Target Weight:** **200 kg**
- **Estimated Weight:** **205.09 kg**

The estimated weight exceeds the target by approximately **5.09 kg**. This deviation can be reduced during the detailed design phase by optimizing non-critical structural members, introducing material cut-outs, or using standard structural sections while maintaining the required strength and stiffness.
